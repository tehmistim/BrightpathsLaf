Neighborhoods in the southside Lafayette area started in the early 1900's and the section Tim's home is in, was built in 1979. The University of Louisiana, Lafayette is also minutes away from Tim's neighborhood.

Tim's favorite restaurant in Lafayette was Ruffino's on the River. Since the pandemic he has yet to return due to the amount of willfully ignorant and selfish individuals bee-bopping around like COVID doesn't exist.
The CajunDome is the local event center that is not utilized enough!
The letters downtown are a new niche festivity that locals repaint for many celebrations from the holidays, movements and symbolic gatherings.
MANY festivals are celebrated in and around Lafayette! CRAWFISH is Tim's favorite festival which is celebrated in Lafayette but the home of the original crawfish festival is in Breaux Bridge, 10 miles east of Lafayette.
